# Kuvera x CRED — Conversion Problem
## Why Kuvera isn’t converting CRED’s premium users

![Kuvera Case](/kuvera_case.png)

### Context
My second PM case study. The Senior PM didn't let me move forward until I could defend every answer.

My first instinct: "Trust issue. Fix the UI."

The pushback: "Trust is a direction. Not a solution."

So I went deeper.

### Problem
The real problem: a segmentation trap.

CRED's 750+ filter doesn't select for investment intent. It creates two opposite users:

### User Segments
| Segment A | Segment B |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Debt-loaded.** Servicing EMIs and home loans. Not looking to invest more. | **Already investing.** On Groww, Zerodha, or with a CA. No reason to migrate. |

55% of CRED users are single-product (bills + rewards only). That's why blanket activation fails.

### Insight (Segmentation Trap)
The deeper issue: CRED built trust around bill payments. Kuvera asks those same users to trust it with a 20-year SIP. No UI fix closes that gap.

### Core Insight Highlight
**"CRED selects for creditworthiness, not investment intent."**

### Solution
Three sequenced interventions:

① **SEBI-verified influencer portfolios (Week 1)**  
SEBI-licensed analysts list real portfolios. Users mirror in one tap. Borrows trust from people users already follow not from CRED's brand.

② **Progressive exposure caps (Month 1)**  
₹10,000 cap per portfolio. Unlocks after every 10 investments. Limits blast radius. Builds trust through behaviour, not promises.

③ **750+ social leaderboard (Month 2–3)**  
Portfolio rankings visible only to other 750+ members. Same psychology as CRED Coins but for wealth. A status signal no other platform can replicate.
